## Color palette chosen

Primary visual palette:
- Deep navy text: trust, contrast, and hospital professionalism
- Soft teal accent: calm operational energy
- Mint / aqua highlights: freshness and softer healthcare tone
- Off-white layered surfaces: minimal, bright, and premium
- Cool gray-blue support text: calm readability without harsh contrast

## Typography decisions

- `Prompt` is used for major headings and section titles to create stronger hierarchy.
- `Sarabun` remains the base reading font for Thai-first body content.
- Headings were shortened and made more direct.
- Supporting copy was intentionally reduced to one short sentence in most areas.

## Hero structure

The hero was redesigned into:
- small brand/navigation row
- large Thai heading explaining the system clearly
- short supporting subtitle
- primary CTA: `เข้าสู่ระบบ`
- secondary CTA: `สมัครใช้งาน`
- compact proof chips for the main system capabilities
- side panel with a lightweight operational summary

## Feature card structure

The feature section now uses 4 concise cards:
- ลงเวลาเวร
- ตรวจสอบรายการ
- รายงานและส่งออก
- สิทธิ์ตามบทบาท

Each card includes:
- icon
- short title
- one concise Thai description

## How the wellness-style inspiration was adapted

Wellness-style inspiration was used only in restrained ways:
- lighter atmosphere
- rounded soft surfaces
- calmer spacing
- subtle elevated cards
- brighter palette

The landing page was still adapted for hospital operations by:
- keeping language practical and Thai-first
- removing consumer-style wellness clichés
- avoiding download / lifestyle / testimonial sections
- focusing on login, register, operational features, and role-based access
- keeping the product message about attendance, review, reporting, and permissions

## Copy simplification decisions

- Long paragraphs from the previous landing page were replaced with shorter supporting lines.
- Feature descriptions were reduced to brief scan-friendly blurbs.
- The page now communicates the system purpose faster and with less reading load.

## Layout decisions

- More whitespace between major sections.
- Cleaner card grouping instead of dense text blocks.
- Hero, features, overview, and final CTA each have one clear responsibility.
- Buttons are visually clearer and more modern without becoming flashy.
